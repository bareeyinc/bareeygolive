The Escrow Settlement Loop

Transacting safely online takes only four simple, friction-free steps.

01

Create & Agree

Establish a contract inside the app outlining price, details, delivery timeframe, and who absorbs the small fee.

02

Lock the Funds

The buyer deposits the transaction amount securely into Bareey Escrow. The seller is instantly notified of the secure hold.

03

Deliver & Inspect

The seller fulfills the service or dispatches the item. The buyer receives and inspects the item for complete satisfaction.

04

Release Payment

Once satisfied, the buyer clicks 'Release'. The seller is paid instantly, withdrawing directly to GTBank, Kuda, Zenith, etc.
